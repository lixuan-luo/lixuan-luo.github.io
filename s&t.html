Science and Technology: Our Road to the Advancement of Mankind

Astronaut Eugene Cernan once proclaimed; “Curiosity is the essence of human existence.” And it is through research and development, or as others may call it, science and technology that we are able to become proficient enough to quench our own thirst for answers. Throughout the centuries, mankind has always spent their time searching for answers, and through that, they have found different ways to improve several aspects of our lives. However, mankind has and will never be satisfied. Our curiosity and thirst for answers will run indefinitely, and with that; the improvement of mankind’s life will run even moreso.

Beginning from the subjects of the human development from a single-celled organism to the development of the universe from a singularity – we have unraveled a colossal amount of mysteries about the world we live in. Consequently, we were able to revolutionize one of the most crucial aspects of our life; our health. The development of medicine has made it possible for mankind to battle a common enemy, which is death. To support this statement, life expectancy at birth has actually drastically increased compared to our earlier existence. This is made possible through the invention of vitamins and medicines and the fact that we are now more knowledgeable about our bodies. In addition, we are now more proficient in battling different pathogens. Before, a lot of people suffered because of the lack of medical care and therefore succumbed to death because of these illnesses.

Aside from medicine– mankind, through science and technology, has also made countless inventions, which could make our lives more effortless and luxurious. When we look around us, we never really think of how the great minds behind every instrument we use have exerted their efforts in order to make our lives into what it is today. Through the invention of the washing machine, television, mops, stoves, oven, refrigerators, and more; our lives at home are way more comfortable than previous generations, take for example the 1800’s. Moreover, through the invention of buses, bicycles, airplanes, personal cars, and more; traveling from one place to another will no longer make us extremely exhausted once we reach our destination and even save more time. Different sectors of our society such as: education, media, medicine, business, governance are all influenced by the advancement of science and technology.

Every piece of machinery we use today, from screws to giant cranes to space stations, are what make mankind accomplish works and tasks more effectively and effortlessly. Ergo, we are now capable of building skyscrapers as high as 830 meters and the smallest molecular machines with the size of 0.7 nanometers. We are now capable of going to places we never thought would have been possible. These achievements include going to the moon, reaching the deepest point of the ocean called the “challenger deep”, and even drilling a hole on earth as deep as 12 kilometers. There are still a lot to unfold about our universe. There are still a lot to improve on the way mankind lives. Mankind will never settle for just one answer, for as we answer one question; even more mind-boggling questions tend to arise. Our capability as humans to even understand the secrets of the universe is truly a gift. It is what makes us different from any other living organism, and if I may quote Uncle Ben from the movie Spiderman; “With great power, comes great responsibility.”
